What’s a VPN Anyway?

A VPN, or Virtual Private Network, is like a secure tunnel for your internet traffic.
It encrypts your connection and hides your IP address, making your online actions
nearly impossible to trace. It's the best way to keep hackers, snoopers, and even
your internet service provider from peeking at what you're up to online.

2. NordVPN
